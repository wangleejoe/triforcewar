About Hero Transform Race

Hero Transform Race is a thrilling obstacle course game where you must switch between different hero forms—runner, swimmer, flyer—to overcome each challenge. Master your timing and reflexes to defeat the course and become the ultimate shape-shifting champion. No downloads, just play instantly online!

Game Features

  • Fast-paced transformation mechanics
  • Colorful and dynamic obstacle courses
  • Runs smoothly on both PC and mobile
  • Free to play - no installation required
